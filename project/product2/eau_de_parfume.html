Libre Eau De Parfum

Description

Libre Eau De Parfum is a captivating and empowering fragrance that was first introduced in 2019. It is a modern and sophisticated scent that celebrates the essence of freedom and self-expression. The fragrance is designed for both women and men who embrace their individuality and are confident in their choices.

Details

Key notes in Libre Eau De Parfum include:

  1. Lavender: Providing a fresh and aromatic opening, lavender adds a touch of elegance and masculinity to the scent.
  2. Orange Blossom: The heart of the fragrance is dominated by orange blossom, which lends a floral and sweet aspect to the perfume.
  3. Musk: As a base note, musk adds depth and sensuality to the fragrance, leaving a long-lasting and memorable impression.
