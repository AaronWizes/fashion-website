Libre Le Parfum

Description

Le Parfum is a fragrance that carries the essence of luxury and elegance. However, it's important to note that there are several YSL fragrances with variations of the name "Le Parfum," such as "Manifesto Le Parfum," "Mon Paris Le Parfum," and others. Each of these fragrances has its unique characteristics and notes.

To provide a general description, "Le Parfum" by YSL is known for its sensuality, sophistication, and lasting appeal. It typically features a harmonious blend of floral, fruity, and oriental notes, resulting in a captivating and memorable scent.

Details

Key notes in Libre Le Parfum include:

  1. Lavender Essence: The fragrance opens with a burst of lavender, creating a fresh and aromatic top note that adds a touch of masculinity to the scent.
  2. Orange Blossom Absolute: At the heart of the perfume, orange blossom provides a floral and sweet facet, offering a sense of femininity and sensuality.
  3. White Tea Extract: As a base note, white tea extract imparts a clean and refreshing quality to the fragrance, making it suitable for everyday wear.
