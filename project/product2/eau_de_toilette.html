Libre Eau De Toilette

Description

Libre Eau De Toilette is a captivating and modern perfume designed for individuals who embrace their independence and confidence. It is a lighter and fresher interpretation of the original Libre fragrance, offering a unique olfactory experience that combines sensuality with a sense of liberation.

Details

Key notes in Libre Eau De Toilette include:

  1. Floral Notes: YSL fragrances frequently incorporate floral notes such as jasmine, rose, and orange blossom, which add a delicate and feminine touch to the scent.
  2. Fruity Accents: Some YSL fragrances feature fruity notes like pear, blackcurrant, or raspberry, which contribute to the fragrance's freshness and vibrancy.
  3. Musk: As a base note, musk adds depth and sensuality to the fragrance, leaving a long-lasting and memorable impression.
